I started playing melee somewhat competitively in the Summer of 2014. It's really exciting being able to play such a nostalgic game so much better than I used to when I was a kid. My absolute favorite thing to do in SSBM is being able to pull off Ken Combos with my main, Marth. You can pull this combo off when the opponent's character is slightly above and in front of you. You forward air them and then immediately jump, move forward and down air them. It feels so powerful and fun to DUNK your opponent ^^
